Blood Bank Management System

Blood Bank Management Systemis designed & suitable for several Blood Bank either operating as individual organization or part of Hospital. It covers all Blood banking process from Donor recruitment, donor management, mobile sessions, component preparation, screening covering all tests, blood stock inventory maintenance, patient registration, cross matching, patient issues etc. Blood donation occurs when a person voluntarily has blood drawn and used for transfusions and/or made into biopharmaceutical medications by a process called fractionation (separation of whole-blood components). Donation may be of whole blood (WB), or of specific components directly (the latter called apheresis). Blood banks often participate in the collection process as well as the procedures that follow it.
